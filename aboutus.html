OUR SERVICES

PR Services

Public Relations is an important part of any marketing strategy in today’s time. With PR services like press releases and pre-written articles and blogs in reputed and well-known newspapers/magazines/websites, one can promote companies, individual work or get editorial coverage.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is an online sales tactic that lets a product owner increase sales by allowing others targeting the same audience i.e. ‘affiliates’ to earn a commission by recommending their product to people. It involves referring a product/service by sharing it on a blog, social media platforms, or website.

Email Marketing

Sometimes traditional approaches can work wonders for your brand. Email Marketing is one such tool where we take the professional approach to marketing and send emails to potential clients/customers to create awareness about your brand. It involves using email to send advertisements, request business, or solicit sales.
